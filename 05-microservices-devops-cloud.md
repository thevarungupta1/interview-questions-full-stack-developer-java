
##  **25 Microservices Interview Questions**

###  **Architecture & Fundamentals**

1. What are microservices? How do they differ from monolithic architecture?
2. What are the main characteristics of a microservice architecture?
3. What is the role of a service registry in microservices?
4. Explain domain-driven design (DDD) and its relevance in microservices.
5. How do you ensure loose coupling between services?
6. What is the difference between API Gateway and Service Mesh?
7. What are common communication patterns (REST, gRPC, messaging) used in microservices?
8. Explain synchronous vs asynchronous communication between services.
9. What is Circuit Breaker pattern? How is it implemented (e.g., Resilience4j, Hystrix)?
10. What is the Saga pattern? Explain choreography vs orchestration.

###  **Design & Deployment**

11. How do you handle inter-service communication failures?
12. How do you achieve configuration management across services?
13. What is service discovery? Tools commonly used (Eureka, Consul, etc.)?
14. How do you manage database per microservice pattern?
15. What is eventual consistency and how do you ensure it?
16. How do you manage versioning of microservice APIs?
17. What are the pros and cons of using an API Gateway?
18. How do you monitor microservices? (Prometheus, Grafana, ELK, Zipkin)
19. How can you secure microservices (JWT, OAuth2, mTLS)?
20. How do you handle distributed logging and tracing?

###  **Scaling & Best Practices**

21. How do you implement fault tolerance in microservices?
22. How do you deploy and scale microservices in Kubernetes or Docker Swarm?
23. How do you test microservices (unit, integration, contract tests)?
24. What is a bounded context? Give an example.
25. How would you design a payment gateway using microservices?
